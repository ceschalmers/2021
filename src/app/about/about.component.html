Who We Are

CES is a student driven society that consists of people aiming to explore the world of entrepreneurship and innovation. We welcome students with dreams of making the world a little bit better, smarter and more sustainable. By arranging workshops, mingles, case nights and competitions we are creating a hub for entrepreneurial students to connect and create.

Our Vision

At CES we want to enable relations between students interested in entrepreneurship while in the meantime provide a connection to the start-up community in Gothenburg. As part of Entrepreneurship By Chalmers, we also aim to lower the bar for entrepreneurship among students and to inspire everyone to use their skills and interests to make their dreams grow.
